# Product requirement

## case study
A boutique workspace agency operates multiple premium workspaces across major cities. Their target audience includes startups, remote teams, and creative professionals looking for high-end, flexible spaces.

The agency relies heavily on visual appeal and frequent content updates to attract clients, but their existing website struggles with performance, content updates, and scalability. 

## Functional requirements

### Home page
- A homepage showcasing an overview of what the platform offers

### Spaces Catalogue
- Location catalogue to view available spaces in countries/cities 
- Ability to filter by cities and size
- Ability to click a workspace to view its details

### Spaces detail
- A page for viewing detailed information about a workspace

### Application
- An application page with a contact form that sends a mail to provider

### Extras
- All pages must include testimonial section at bottom
- All pages to include the apply button for easy navigation to contact page

### CMS
- A content management CMS for staff to edit contents on the platform.


## Non-functional requirements

### Performance contraints
- SEO as a first-class system
- LCP < 2.5s
- CLS < 0.1
- INP < 200ms
- JS budget - unknown
- Images - Lazy loaded
- Perceived performance
- Safe content updates
- Graceful degradation
- Observability for ux
- International expansion
